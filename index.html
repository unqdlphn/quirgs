About Quirgs

[In Development]

Welcome to Quirgs! Your new open-source hub for concise, practical Quick reference guides on technical topics, including AI workflows, SOPs, flowcharts and more. No ads, no spam, just guides.

Every developer knows the frustration of digging through lengthy documentation to find that one command or parameter they need. Quirgs was born from this shared experience - the belief that reference materials should be:

  • Scannable- Find what you need in seconds
  • Comprehensive- Cover all the essential information
  • Beautiful- Designed with care and attention to detail
  • Accessible - Available wherever you need them

Each one is carefully crafted to provide essential information in an easily digestible format, helping you learn and implement new technologies faster. The information is gathered by humans and LLMs, and then distilled into a quick reference format. Some of the guides are based on community requests, ensuring they stay relevant to your needs.

We aim to cover a wide range of topics. Whether you're a developer, data scientist, or just someone interested in technology, we have something for you. Quirgs!

Stay tuned for more guides!
