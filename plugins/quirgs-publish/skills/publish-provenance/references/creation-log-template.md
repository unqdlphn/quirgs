# Creation Log Template

Standard format for documenting the creative process of a music release. Complete this log at the time of creation for maximum credibility.

---

## When to Complete This Log

- **Immediately after finishing a track** — while details are fresh
- **Before releasing** — a pre-release log is more credible than a retrospective one
- **When AI tools were used** — even for minor uses (Tier 1 tools like AI mastering)
- **For any catalog intended for sync licensing** — supervisors and publishers may ask for provenance documentation during due diligence

## Abbreviated Log (Tier 0–1 Only)

For works with no AI-generated content or Tier 1 AI tools only, a shorter log is acceptable:

```
CREATION LOG (ABBREVIATED — TIER 0/1)
Track:       [Title] | ISRC: [Value]
Creator:     [Name]
Date:        [YYYY-MM-DD]
AI tools:    [None / Tier 1 tools: list]
Human authorship: [Brief summary — melody, lyrics, arrangement, performance]
Log by:      [Name] on [Date]
```

---

## Retention Recommendation

Retain creation logs for a minimum of:
- **10 years** from release date for actively licensed works
- **Life of copyright** for catalog works with significant commercial value
- **Indefinitely** for works registered with a PRO or subject to an ongoing license

Store logs with the master session files in the same backup system. A log that can't be found is of limited value.
