## What it does

The ISO 42001 Audit Prep skill guides teams through a clause-by-clause readiness assessment against ISO/IEC 42001:2023 — the international standard for AI management systems (AIMS). For each clause, it produces a control status (implemented / partial / not implemented), identifies the audit evidence that a third-party auditor will look for, flags gaps, and assigns a control owner. The output is a structured gap analysis report that can serve as your internal audit workpaper or as preparation material before an external certification audit.

## Frameworks covered

- ISO/IEC 42001:2023 — AI Management System standard (all clauses 4–10)
- ISO/IEC 42002 — AI management system implementation guidance (supplementary)
- ISO 9001:2015 — referenced by 42001 for management system structure (HLS)

## When to use it

Use this skill when your organization is preparing for ISO/IEC 42001 certification — either as a self-assessment ahead of an external audit, or as an ongoing internal audit tool. It's also valuable when leadership needs a structured view of where the AIMS stands before committing to a certification timeline, or when a new AI system is added to scope and the team needs to assess whether existing controls cover it. Unlike a quick multi-framework checklist, this skill performs clause-level depth — it produces the kind of documented evidence review an auditor will actually examine.

CLAUSE 6 — PLANNING
