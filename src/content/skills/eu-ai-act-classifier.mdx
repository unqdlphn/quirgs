## What it does

The EU AI Act Classifier takes a plain-language description of your AI system and returns a definitive risk tier under the EU AI Act — prohibited, high-risk (Annex III), GPAI, limited-risk, or minimal-risk — along with the specific legal basis for that classification. It then surfaces the exact obligations that attach to your tier and deployment role (provider, deployer, importer, or distributor), so you know precisely what compliance work is required before you ship.

## Frameworks covered

- EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
- Annex III — high-risk AI system categories
- Article 6 — classification rules for high-risk AI
- Article 5 — prohibited AI practices
- Article 3 — definitions (GPAI, provider, deployer)

## When to use it

Reach for this skill at the start of any new AI project or before deploying an existing system in the EU market. It's the right first step before running any other compliance tool — you need to know your tier before you can know your obligations. Also use it when your system's purpose, training data, or deployment context changes in a material way, since tier classification is fact-specific and shifts with the system.

