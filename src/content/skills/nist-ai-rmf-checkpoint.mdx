## What it does

The NIST AI RMF Checkpoint walks your AI system through a structured, stage-scoped assessment against the four core functions of the NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 — Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage. It produces a risk profile that identifies which controls are addressed, which are missing, and what the NIST AI RMF Playbook recommends as next actions. The output is scoped to your project lifecycle stage, so you get relevant controls — not a kitchen-sink checklist.

## Frameworks covered

- NIST AI RMF 1.0 (January 2023)
- NIST AI RMF Playbook (subcategory-level actions)
- NIST AI RMF Organizational Profiles (generic and sector-specific)

## When to use it

Use this skill when your organization needs to evaluate AI risk against a federal or enterprise standard — particularly when working with U.S. government agencies, federal contractors, or any organization that has adopted the AI RMF as a governance baseline. It's also the right tool mid-project when your team wants to understand which risk controls are in place before moving to the next lifecycle stage, or when preparing for an internal governance review.
